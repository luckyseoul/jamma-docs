# JAMMA Standard Pinout

Canonical 56-pin edge connector (Parts Side numbered 1–28, Solder Side lettered A–f).

**Primary reference**: [Mike's Arcade JAMMA Pinout](http://www.mikesarcade.com/cgi-bin/spies.cgi?action=url&page=Jamma.html&type=pinout)

### Summary Groups
- **Power**: Multiple GND, +5V (heavy), +12V, -5V
- **Video**: Red, Green, Blue, Composite Sync, Video Ground
- **Audio**: Speaker + / Speaker -
- **Controls**: P1/P2 Start, 8-way joystick (or 4-way), Buttons 1–3 (extendable)
- **Coin / Service**: Coin A/B, Counters, Lockout, Service, Test, Tilt/Slam

Many games implement only a subset. Extra buttons (4–6) almost always require an additional harness ("Kick" or JAMMA+).

Full dual-side table and notes will be expanded here. Always verify against the specific board's manual or community pinout, as non-standard implementations exist.
